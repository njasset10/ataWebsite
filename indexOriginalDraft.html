ATA is a tuition-free, independent school serving 5th through 8th grade students from under-resourced backgrounds in Boston. Named for the first Black American to graduate from college in the U.S. and the only Black American to be elected to a state legislature prior to the Civil War, Alexander Twilight academy offers the same quality of education, or better, to its students as is provided by elite private elementary and middle schools. Alexander Twilight Academy will commence full-time academic operation in Fall 2019.
