Heart - Humility - Hustle

Alexander Twilight Academy (ATA) is a tuition-free, independent school serving 5th through 8th grade students from under-resourced backgrounds in Boston. Named after Alexander Twilight, the first Black American to graduate from college in the U.S. and the only Black American to be elected to a state legislature prior to the Civil War, ATA offers the same quality of education, or better, to its students as is provided by elite private elementary and middle schools. ATA will commence full-time academic operations in Fall 2019 with a 5th grade class.


If We...

  • Provide our students with a rigorous, engaging, and holistic education
  • Build a community where relationships amongst students, faculty, and families are the cornerstone of our educational philosophy
  • Make every student feel valued, cared about, and known
  • Nurture students’ passions and intellectual curiosities
  • Expose students to new worlds
  • Empower the development of 21st-century skills such as creativity, collaboration, communication, critical thinking, and problem-solving
  • Prioritize the continued leadership development of our students by building upon their existing strengths and assets

Then Our Students Will...

  • Be equipped with the academic and non-cognitive skills to gain access to limitless opportunities and pathways for success and to thrive at elite high schools, colleges, and in the 21st-century workforce
  • Become lifelong learners, who live rich and meaningful lives
  • Feel empowered as leaders who will actively seek to make the world a better place
  • Realize and fulfill their dreams and limitless potential
